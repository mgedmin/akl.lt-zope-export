ROADSHOW

eVitamins Roadshow – for raising ICT awareness and computer literacy

The idea is to travel around Lithuania's rural areas with all the team by a special bus visiting about 40 towns. At each location we will organize 3-days seminars “giving” each type of eVitamins that we can offer now (see eVitamins section). At each place we will organize installfests to help people learn to install open source operating systems and software in their computers. Also - provide technical consulting and find one or two partners that will be trained to provide technical support for local communities, SMEs, schools and local governments.

Roadshow is going to be a huge one year project that need partnership to build a consorcium.

If you are interested in participating in an “eVitamins Roadshow” consorcium, please, contact us by e-mail or telephone: +370 5 210 4005.

The idea of “eVitamins Roadshow” for the first time was presented at WITFOR event on 27-29 August, 2003 in Vilnius.
