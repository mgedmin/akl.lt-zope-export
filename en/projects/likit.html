Lithuanian Language in Information Technologies
(LIKIT -- Lietuvių Kalba Informacinėse Technologijose)

2001

The aim of LIKIT project was to analyze problems of Lithuanian language in computers' usage, to provide useful information and consultation about using Lithuanian language, its' standards in Information Technologies.

The first activities concerning open source software in Lithuania were launched at the end of 2001 by the Information Society Development Committee to the Government of Lithuania. GO „Infostruktūra“ made analyze of possibilities to localize open source programs, and a pilot translation of Internet program „Mozilla“ was made. The results of investigation are published and being renewed at www.likit.lt.

People behing this were a professional team of specialists from Mathematics and Informatics Institute and Vilnius University Mathematics Faculty.
