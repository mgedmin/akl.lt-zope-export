Presentation for school IT teachers

November, 2002

Presentation „Perspectives on using localized open source software in the secondary schools of Lithuania“ was held at annual conference of IT teachers of Lithuania. Conference took place in Klaipėda on the 9th of November, 2002. There were about 300 attendees at the conference. Seminar was given by AKL member Tomas Jonušas. Teachers were impressed.
