Presentations for local authorities

September, October, 2002

Seminar for Šiauliai county local authorities about use of open source software and open data standards in public sector. Seminar was initiated and held by AKL members Tomas Jonušas and Mantas Kriaučiunas.

Seminar on Perspectives of use of open source software in the sector of public authority was held in Druskinininkai at the conference of Information society development in Lithuanian municipalities on the 14th of October, 2002. More than 60 attendees from all Lithuanian municipalities participated in the seminar. Seminar was given by AKL member Tomas Jonuas at his initiative .
